ABOUT

our
journey...

HiBlend Health Bar & Cafe's concept has been created by three brothers, Miles and Joey Sugahara, and Jason Chang. Two brothers by blood, one built on the brotherhood and bond in the making since elementary school days.

Fascination with clean and healthy living, simply wanting fresh tasty ingredients set us off into a journey into local farms all over the island. Almost two years ago, when we first set sail on the mission to obtain the best, most affordable ingredients to provide for our clientele. We've physically walked the soils of farms, many dead end phone calls were made, waiting lists which never seem to ever come up, no return calls, and slim pickings in variety of produce needed. Never giving up making connections and gathering information at multiple farmers markets. More

Then one day a friend's information on a farm doing no spray organic practice led us back to our old stomping grounds of Kaiser High School. The farm is located in the back of the high school, rand is run by Bill and Karian, named Wally's Farm. This was a dream come true to be able to work with them for over a year, participating in recycling ourcomposts and coffee grounds weekly, harvesting our own produce 3-4 days a week, and helping to clean the farm when needed. We are very thankful for the relationship we've built with Bill and Karian. As we went on unfortunately our demands grew and we knew we had to expand.

Most recently we began working with the wonderful Kahumana Organic Farm in Waianae which we are very pleased with their fresh produce and service. Its been a long journey and we have learned a lot. We have grown as a business as well as people and we are very proud of what we have accomplished to this day. It definitely has been a battle of staying true to ourselves and concept to carry out what we believe in. Understanding and learning what it truly means to support local for a better more sustainable Hawaii. We do outsource for certain produce due to the limited resources here on the islands. Some produce just can't be obtained locally or some costs are just too high to make it as a local business. We truly do our best to offer our people a variety of local, farm fresh, organic, all natural, and non GMO ingredients. Less
